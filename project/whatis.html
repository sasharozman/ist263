What is a Tiny House?

According to the 2020 United States Census Bureau, the average American single-family home is around 2,300 square feet. While there is no official definition of a tiny house, it is typically between 100 and 400 square feet. A tiny house is rarely over 500 square feet.

What is the Tiny House Movement?

Simply put, the Tiny House Movement is a social movement that encourages living a more simplistic lifestyle. Living in a larger home and the cost associated with it, for many, is a detriment to their happiness. Thus, people have turned to tiny homes to lessen the emotional and financial burden of stuff and embrace the freedoms that come with the lifestyle.
